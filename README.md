# Emotional Petri Dish
> Observe. Record. Reflect.

Emotional Petri Dish is a project designed to help users observe, record, and reflect on their emotions.
The project encourages emotional awareness through simple daily observations. By recording thoughts and emotions over time, users can better recognize emotional patterns, understand their responses, and reflect on personal growth.

### Features
* Record daily emotions and observations
* Review emotion history
* Organize emotional records in a clear interface
* Store data locally in the browser (no account required)
* Privacy-friendly design — your data stays on your own device

### Getting Started
1. Download this repository.
2. Open in your web browser.
3. Start recording your emotions.

No installation or internet connection is required after downloading.

### Privacy
Emotional Petri Dish does **not** collect or upload any personal data.

All records are stored locally in your browser, giving you complete control over your own information.
